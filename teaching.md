# Teaching Activities

## Undergraduate Teaching (BHMS)

### First BHMS
- Basic concepts of Homoeopathy
- Introduction to Case Taking
- Fundamentals of Materia Medica

### Second BHMS
- Homoeopathic Repertory
- Rubric identification and repertorisation exercises
- Boericke Repertory training

### Third BHMS
- Advanced repertorial approaches
- Kent Repertory
- Clinical case analysis

### Final BHMS
- Boger Synoptic Key (BSK)
- Boenninghausen's Therapeutic Pocket Book
- Phatak Repertory
- Clinical repertorisation

## Teaching Philosophy

Emphasis on:
- Conceptual understanding
- Clinical application
- Case-based learning
- Research-oriented thinking
