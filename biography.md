# Dr. Anandkumar Patel

## Academic Profile

I am Dr. Anandkumar Patel, Faculty Member and PhD Scholar in the Department of Repertory.

My academic interests include Homoeopathic Repertory, Clinical Research, Medical Education and development of learning resources for Homoeopathic students.

## Research Scholar

## Teaching Areas

- Homoeopathic Repertory
- Case Taking and Analysis
- Materia Medica and Clinical Application
- Research Methodology

## Academic Vision

To promote scientific learning, clinical reasoning and research-oriented education in Homoeopathy.
